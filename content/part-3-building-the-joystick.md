Arthur Sacek’s joystick was programmed so that multiple motors on Elles could be controlled by a single motor on the joystick. We initially considered doing this as well, but because we wanted to prioritize user control, each motor on our joystick controls only one of Elles’ motors at a time.

![Diagram of Joystick](/notion-images/part-3--building-the-joystick/3.svg)

Diagram of Joystick

We created two joystick modes in order to allow for as much movement as possible. Although not all of Elles’ motors can be run using the joystick, being able to switch between different body parts significantly increased our ability to control the robot.
