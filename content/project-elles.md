# Project Elles

# Who is Elles?

Elles is a robot originally created by Arthur Sacek from LEGO Education. Consisting of 18 motors, 4 SPIKE Prime hubs, and 2 SPIKE Essentials, Elles has the potential to simulate human movement and is able to perform a wide variety of actions, from waving to dancing.

# Project Goals:

- Make the original BrickLink model easier to follow visually
- Create BrickLink instructions
- Build Elles using our instructions
- Program Elles and be able to control her with a joystick (BLE connection)
